Project Analysis

Client: Fat Wreck Chords

Project: Website Re-Design

Designer: Keith Barker

Site Intent

The sites intent is to promote new music and to help new bands become noticed in the music industry. You can also check out tour dates, buy albums, clothing and other accessories. The site is currently fully funcional that allows the user to navigate to which ever page they want to find out information about Fat Wreck Chords.

The goal of the re-design is to clean up the text and adjust the background colors that allows for better readability. Some of the information will also be rearranged and grouped into categories that will be linked to just that information. The goal is to clean up the site so that the content is easy on the users eye.

Competitive Analysis

When looking at competitors to Fat Wreck Chords, it is important to understand what the competitor offers differently from Fat Wreck Chords. Do they get more visitors to their websites? Do they offer more services? Are they a top company for bands to sign on to their label?

Third Man Records

The site at www.thirdmanrecords.com is clean and well laid out. They have signed big names such as The White Stripes and The Raconteurs. There targeted audience is similar to Fat Wreck Chords. They offer record label contracts as well.

www.fat-cat.co.uk

The site at www.fat-cat.co.uk is clean and well laid out. Each section is marked with a black background and white text. Fat Cat Records have both US and UK locations, which expands its opportunity for more people to visit their site.

www.defected.com

The site at www.defected.com is pleasing to the eye and each section is laid out and flows well with the page. They have a wide range of music genre that would benefit Fat Wreck Chords in the music industry.

Targeted Audience

Fat Wreck Chords targets new bands that are looking to have their music played in clubs and to possibly have their albums signed under Fat Wreck Chords label. The targeted audience is from ages 18-34.
